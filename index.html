Payment Plan for Berkeley at Dubai Hills Estate

At Berkeley by Soho Development, we understand that purchasing a home is a significant financial commitment, and we are dedicated to making this process as seamless and accessible as possible. To ensure that owning your dream apartment at Dubai Hills Estate is within reach, we offer a flexible and convenient payment plan designed to suit various financial needs. The payment structure typically starts with a manageable initial down payment upon booking your unit, which secures your residence and provides peace of mind.

This is followed by a series of construction-linked installments that are spread out over the development period, allowing you to make incremental payments as the project progresses. Finally, upon completion and handover of your apartment, the remaining balance is due, ensuring that your investment is both gradual and well-paced. Additionally, we offer attractive post-handover payment options, allowing you to continue making payments even after moving into your new home.

This phased approach ensures financial flexibility and eases the burden of large, upfront payments. With our thoughtfully designed payment plan, your journey to owning a luxurious residence at Berkeley becomes a straightforward and stress-free experience.
